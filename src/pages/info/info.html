IEEE SLSYWC 2017

On Dec. 22, 23 and 24th At Sabaragamuwa University

The IEEE Sri Lanka Section, for the sixth consecutive year, is getting ready to present the Sri Lankan IEEE enthusiasts with yet another memorable episode of the Sri Lanka Section Student / Young Professional / Women in Engineering Congress for the year of 2017. This is undoubtedly the most awaited event of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section where Student Branches,Young Professionals and Women in Engineering share their achievements to receive public endorsement and inspire others. Starting in 2012, it has been a long journey in bringing the congress to the stratum it enjoys today and the section expects to reap more and more with each year. This year the congress is themed on “VISION BEYOND TECHNOLOGY” and IEEE SLSYWC’17 will consist of various interesting sessions to facilitate the theme.
